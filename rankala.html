About Rankala

Rankala is one of the best place in Kolhapur..
its a biggest lake in kolhapur.
Rankala is surrounded by beautiful parks. Rankala Lake Overview. Rankala Lake was built by the Chhatrapati rulers of Kolhapur. It is believed that the lake is directly connected to a well located in the Panhala Fort. Other attractions present near the lake are the chariot of Lord Shiva and a temple with a structure of Nandi (bull).
The Padpath Udyan that is built close to the Rankala Lake offers various recreational facilities like lake side walk. Apart from this,the lake is also well-known among the visitors for different activities like boating and horse riding.
